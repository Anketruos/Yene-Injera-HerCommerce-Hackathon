About YeneInjera

Learn more about our mission and vision

Our Story

YeneInjera was founded in 2023 with a simple mission: to connect traditional injera makers with modern consumers while preserving the rich cultural heritage of Ethiopian cuisine.

Our Mission

We empower women entrepreneurs in the injera business by providing them with digital tools to reach more customers, increase their income, and grow their businesses sustainably.

Our Vision

To become the leading platform for authentic Ethiopian injera, connecting communities while celebrating and preserving traditional cooking methods.
